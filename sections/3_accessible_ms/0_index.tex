\chapter{A Magidor Forcing with Collapses}
\label{accessible_ms}

Section \ref{ms:ms} covered prior results on mutual stationarity principles at $\aleph_\omega$.
To push mutual stationarity principles down to larger accessible cardinals, especially of uncountable cofinality, we want tools to singularize a large cardinal $\kappa$ while also collapsing below to make $\kappa$ into $\aleph_\lambda$ for some $\lambda$.

To that end, we now describe a Magidor-type forcing with interleaved guided collapses $\mathbb{MC}\left(\vec{U}\right)$ that singularizes a measurable cardinal $\kappa$ to cofinality $\lambda$ and collapses cardinals below.
As an additional result, we state and prove Prikry-type lemmata (\thref{magidor_collapse_prikry_open_dense} and \thref{magidor_collapse_prikry_open_dense_better}) and prove a characterization of genericy for $\mathbb{MC}\left(\vec{U}\right)$ in \thref{magidor_collapse_generic_implies_geometric} and \thref{magidor_collapse_generic_iff_geometric}.

In the event $\lambda=\omega_1$, we will further get that $\kappa$ becomes the new $\aleph_{\omega_1}$.
